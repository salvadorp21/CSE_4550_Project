Achieve a Lean Physique

A lean physique combines low body fat with well-defined muscle tone, often achieved through a balance of targeted exercises and a disciplined diet. This guide provides a comprehensive plan to reach and maintain a lean body.

Diet Plan for a Lean Physique

Achieving a lean physique requires a diet that focuses on high-quality protein, complex carbs, and healthy fats. This diet emphasizes nutrient-dense, low-calorie foods to help build muscle and reduce body fat.

  • Protein: Chicken, fish, eggs, legumes, and tofu to support muscle growth.
  • Carbohydrates: Whole grains, oats, sweet potatoes for sustained energy.
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, olive oil, nuts to promote satiety and hormone balance.
  • Hydration: At least 2-3 liters of water per day for optimal metabolic function.
