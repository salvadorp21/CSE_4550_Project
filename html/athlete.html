Athlete Training Program

Achieve an optimal athlete physique with this comprehensive diet and workout plan.

Athlete Physique

The ideal athlete physique focuses on balanced muscle development, low body fat, and agility. This program is designed to build a lean, powerful, and functional body.

Body Fat %

10-12%

Muscle Mass

Well-balanced upper and lower body

Height

Varies based on sport

Diet Plan

Fuel your body with the right nutrients for energy and recovery. This plan includes a balanced intake of proteins, carbs, and healthy fats.

  • Protein: 1.2-2 grams per kilogram of body weight
  • Carbohydrates: 3-5 grams per kilogram of body weight
  • Fats: 20-30% of total daily calories
  • Hydration: 3-4 liters of water daily

Workout Plan

This workout routine is designed to improve strength, endurance, and agility. Each week combines strength training, cardio, and flexibility exercises.

Monday

Strength Training: Full body, 45-60 minutes

Cardio: 20-30 minutes HIIT

Wednesday

Strength Training: Upper body, 45 minutes

Flexibility: 15 minutes stretching

Friday

Strength Training: Lower body, 45 minutes

Cardio: 30 minutes running
